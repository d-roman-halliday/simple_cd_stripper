About

The Jukebox

This is inspired by a Sound Leisure Lime-Light CD jukebox (which olds 80 CDs): Sound Leisure im Archiv Jukebox-World

Label space

  • Track Listing width: 7.5cm
  • Track Listing height: 10.5cm
  • Plus spaces for the CD book/cover art

General specs of this jukebox

  • 21st Century Mechanism
  • Lightest wallmount jukebox available holding 80 discs
  • Most compact dimensions of any wallbox holding 80 discs
  • 80 discs, 80 displays
  • Mechanism completely enclosed by its own dust cover to prevent dirt and dust on disc's
  • Low level coin mech entry
  • Internal fitting Slave Amp and CDV decoder, 80 disk 80 display, 24hr real time clock (BGM)
  • Random play via keyboard
  • Twin Channel amp
  • Spare position for a Slave Amp Mic input, Aux Input /Aux Output
  • Faulty disc diagnostic
  • Simple popularity read out via LED display
  • Drives all Sound Leisure wall boxes
  • Stand available
  • Track lockout facility
  • H1020 x W638 x D310 mm; 60 kg
